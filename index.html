Our Vision
Fusion Conference is dedicated to teaching, equipping, and empowering students to live out their greatest potential, and to move forward in their relationship with Christ. Our focus is worship, self-growth, and having the best times of our lives. It's an experience you'll never forget!

Where it Began
Fusion Conference was birthed in the hearts of our youth staff in the Winter of 2018. Our goal was to create an environment for students to know God, bring their friends, grow deeper in their personal relationship with Christ, while equipping them to reach their campuses and friends.

Students
At Fusion Conference students will play loud & crazy games, serve others in services projects, eat tons of delicious food & get to know other students. Our Host Houses will help them build lifelong friendships while giving them that all important sleeping spot. Youth Pastors/Leaders
Fusion Conference for Student Pastors/Leaders is designed to help students and leaders connect inside their youth ministry, making them stronger and healthier. The goal is for student pastors and students to grow closer together as they grow closer to God. The tools given over this high energy, spirit packed weekend will set a foundation for your students to build on when they return to their church and school campus!
